ABOUT DM WATCH

ABOUT US

DM WATCH was established in 2013 under the name Disaster Management Watch. Since its establishment, it has been involved in consultancy, research, and training engaging a group of young professionals with diverse backgrounds. Envisioning to contribute in the development of the society and the country as a whole, DM WATCH has been working with various partners from both public and private sector. Among these United Nations Development Programme, IMED, Local Government Engineering Department, Urban Development Directorate, SNV, Save the Children, PLAN International, Concern Worldwide are only to name a few.
Currently, DM WATCH is working in several thematic areas such as Environment, Climate Change, Agriculture and Natural Resources, Urban Development and Management, WASH and Public Health, Public Policy and Governance. The organization strives for excellence in research and consultancy services. It consists of a pool of dedicated professionals with commendable expertise working in the aforementioned thematic areas. Depending on their knowledge, expertise, and effort, DM WATCH has successfully completed more than 50 assignments so far.
DM WATCH is working to contribute in the creation of a sustainable and desirable future through quality research and consultancy in partnership with government agencies, donor agencies, INGOs, local NGOs, and various institutions.Continue Reading.
